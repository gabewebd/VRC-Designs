About Me
My design philosopy
Raphael believes in creating spaces that are not only functional but also deeply connected to their context and the people who will inhabit them. He strives to create spaces that are both beautiful and meaningful, reflecting a harmonious blend of form and function.
My design process
Raphael's design process begins with a deep understanding of the client's needs and the site's unique characteristics. He then explores various design concepts, incorporating sustainable practices and innovative solutions, before refining the design through detailed drawings and 3D renderings.
